## Design Notes

### Color Implementation
- **Hero Overlay:** Black at 50% opacity over photo
- **Text on Overlay:** Pure white (#ffffff) for maximum contrast
- **Section Backgrounds:** Your dark theme (#1e1e1e)
- **Cards:** Subtle elevation (#252525)
- **Accent Colors:** Blue (#4a9eff) for CTAs

### Interactive Elements
- **Hover Effects:** Subtle scale/opacity changes
- **Smooth Scrolling:** Between sections
- **Image Lazy Loading:** Performance optimization
- **Responsive Design:** Mobile-first approach

### Content Strategy
- **Hero:** Immediate impact with professional photo
- **Portfolio:** Quantified results and visual proof
- **About:** Personal connection and credibility
- **Contact:** Clear call-to-action
